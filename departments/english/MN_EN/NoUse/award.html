Pride Awards

Year 2014

Outstanding Alumnus Award 2013 (Nursing Education)

Assistant Professor Dr. Duangrat Wattanakitkrileart, faculty member of the Department of Medical Nursing, Faculty of Nursing, Mahidol University, received the Outstanding Alumnus Award 2013 in Nursing Education from the Siriraj Nurses' Alumni Association.

Year 2013

Outstanding Teacher Award, Faculty of Nursing, Mahidol University

Assistant Professor Dr. Duangrat Wattanakitkrileart, Department of Medical Nursing, Faculty of Nursing, Mahidol University, received the Outstanding Teacher Award for the academic year 2012 from the Faculty of Nursing, Mahidol University.

Year 2012

Outstanding Civil Servant, Employee, and University Staff Award (Faculty Level: Academic Position)

Dr. Wichuda Charoenkitkarn, Assistant Dean for Education, Department of Medical Nursing, received the Outstanding Civil Servant, Employee, and University Staff Award (Faculty Level - Faculty of Nursing, Mahidol University) for the year 2011, under the University Staff category (Academic Position), at the 43rd Anniversary of the Royal Bestowal of Mahidol University Name Ceremony on March 2, 2012, at the Office of the President, Mahidol University, Salaya.

Year 2011

Outstanding Siriraj Nurse Alumnus Award 2010 (Nurse Educator Category)

Associate Professor Dr. Kanaungnit Pongthavornkamol, Department of Medical Nursing, received the Outstanding Siriraj Nurse Alumnus Award 2010 in Nursing Education (Nurse Educator Category) from the Siriraj Nurses' Alumni Association on February 12, 2011.

Model Teacher Award of the Faculty Senate 2010

Associate Professor Dr. Kanaungnit Pongthavornkamol, Department of Medical Nursing, received the Model Teacher Award of the Faculty Senate 2010 from the Mahidol University Faculty Senate at the 42nd Anniversary of the Royal Bestowal of Mahidol University Name, 123rd Anniversary of Mahidol University Ceremony on March 2, 2011.

Year 2010

Outstanding Siriraj Nurse Alumnus Award 2009 (Nurse Educator Category)

Assistant Professor Dr. Wanpen Pinyopasakul, Department of Medical Nursing, received the Outstanding Siriraj Nurse Alumnus Award 2009 in Nursing Education (Nurse Educator Category) from the Siriraj Nurses' Alumni Association.

Model Teacher Award of the Faculty Senate 2009

Assistant Professor Dr. Wanpen Pinyopasakul, Department of Medical Nursing, received the Model Teacher Award of the Faculty Senate 2009 from the Mahidol University Faculty Senate at the Royal Bestowal of Mahidol University Name Ceremony on March 2, 2010.

Outstanding Teacher Award, Faculty of Nursing

Associate Professor Dr. Kanaungnit Pongthavornkamol, Department of Medical Nursing, Faculty of Nursing, Mahidol University, received the Outstanding Teacher Award 2009 from the Faculty of Nursing, Mahidol University.

Year 2009

Outstanding Teacher Award, Faculty of Nursing

Assistant Professor Dr. Wanpen Pinyopasakul, Department of Medical Nursing, received the Outstanding Teacher Award 2008 from the Faculty of Nursing, Mahidol University.

Outstanding Alumna Award (Academic Executive Category)

Assistant Professor Dr. Aurawamon Sriyuktasuth, Assistant Dean for Graduate Studies and International Relations, received an honorary plaque as an Outstanding Alumna appointed to an academic executive role from the Thai Red Cross College of Nursing Alumni Association on August 10, 2009.

Outstanding Nurse Award (Nurse Educator Category)

Assistant Professor Dr. Wanpen Pinyopasakul, Department of Medical Nursing, received the Outstanding Nurse Award 2009 in Nursing Education (Nurse Educator category) from the Thailand Nursing and Midwifery Council.
