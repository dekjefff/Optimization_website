WELCOME TO OUR DEPARTMENT

          Department of Obstetric and Gynecological Nursing is a professional leader in academy, research, and service of midwifery and maternal-newborn and families, including women’s health. The Department is also responsible for all academic endeavors leading to the excellence in the care of maternity, newborn, and midwifery under the administration of Head of Department. There are total of 31 qualified faculty members and a staff categorized as follows:

      • 4 associate professors,
      • 10 assistant professors,
      • 16 instructors, and
      • 1 administrative staff
