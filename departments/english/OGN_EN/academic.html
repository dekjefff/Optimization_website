Academic Services
 

          The faculty members in our Department directly provide the quality professional services to the society through academic projects and training programs. A guideline for the provision of the academic services is set up by the Department of Obstetric Gynecological nursing as followed:
 
Serving as an academic expertise:
  • Lecturer for students in other educational institutes
  • Speaker in nursing conference, seminar, and training for nurses, caregivers, and other people
  • Editor and reviewer for Professional Journals
  • External committee of thesis/dissertation in other educational institutions
  • Expert reviwer for academic position
Providing several projects of health promotion to specific groups of people as follows:
  • Pregnant women through the childbirth preparation project
  • Female teenagers through the sex education and Sexual transmitted diseases prevention project
  • Thai couples through the Preconception advice project-People through telephone counselling project
Serving as a committee of professional organizations as follows:
  • Thailand Nurse Council
  • Thai Nursing Association
  • Society of Maternal-Newborn and Nurse-Midwives of Thailand
  • Siriraj Nursing Alumni
  • WHO Collaboration Centre for Nursing and Miswifery Development
