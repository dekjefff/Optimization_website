Academic Services

For academic services within the University, instructors at the Department of Mental Health and Psychiatric Nursing has initiated projects to promote mental health and screen mental health problems of students in baccalaureate program.  The Department also provides counseling to students of the Faculty of Nursing and also students from other schools within the University.  Additionally, the Department has also joined the Student and Special Affairs and Academic Services of the Faculty to provide counseling services in special events organized by the Faculty and the University.

For community services, instructors of the Department of Mental Health and Psychiatric Nursing provide counseling to students and parents at various private and government schools as well as giving consultation to teachers at the schools in taking care of students with academic difficulty and mental health problems.  Our instructors are also invited to provide special lectures on various issues related to mental health promotion, care for patients with chronic diseases with mental health problems, patients with psychiatric problems, and rehabilitation of patients with psychiatric problems in both hospitals and communities.
