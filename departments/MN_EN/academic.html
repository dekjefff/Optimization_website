Academic Services

Academic Services Policy

The Department of Medical Nursing also has a policy in reinforcing the staffs to provide educational services in many aspects. Examples of educational service activities are as follows:

  • • Be a guest speaker for other departments within the Faculty or in other institutions.
  • • Provide educational services thorough public.
  • • Be an expert validating research instruments or assessing performance of nurse specialist applicants.
  • • Provide educational services for lay people regularly.
  • • Participate in mobile clinic services in association with the Siriraj Nurse Alumni Association.
