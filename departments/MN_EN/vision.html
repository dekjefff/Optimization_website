Vision / Mission

Vision

To be a leader in medical nursing education and research in Thailand and Southeast Asia

Mission

  • Offer medical nursing courses for baccalaureate and master's programs
  • Conduct research in medical nursing including educational and clinical area
  • Provide academic services in medical nursing to various groups
  • Support and participate in Thai cultural activities
  • Initiate collaborative research and academic networking in medical nursing with international institutions
