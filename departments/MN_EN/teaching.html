Teaching

Instructors of the Medical Nursing Department are mainly responsible for organizing teaching-learning activities, both theory and practice, for Bachelor of Nursing Science. In addition, the department is also responsible for organizing those activities for nursing students in other programs including the doctoral program, master program in relation to adult nursing, and specialty program such as medical nursing in chronic illnesses, cardio-thoracic nursing, cancer nursing, infection control nursing; AIDS and nursing management (a 4 - month course).
