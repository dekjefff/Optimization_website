Department News (2022)

International Conference: Challenges in NCDs management to achieve the SDGs in the COVID-19 era and beyond

January 10, 2022

On January 10, 2022, the Faculty of Nursing, Mahidol University, in collaboration with the Siriraj Nurses' Alumni Association under the Royal Patronage of Her Royal Highness Princess Srinagarindra, and the Department of Nursing, Siriraj Hospital, organized the online International Conference "Challenges in NCDs management to achieve the SDGs in the COVID-19 era and beyond." In this event, Assoc. Prof. Dr. Aurawamon Sriyuktasuth was a guest speaker on the topic "Communication to increase adherence," and Instructor Nattaya Praha served as the moderator.
