ECG Interpretation

Registered Nurse Training: ECG Interpretation and Nursing Care

Department of Medical Nursing

On May 17th-19th, 2017, Department of Medical Nursing, Faculty of Nursing, Mahidol University held the training for Registered Nurse (RN) under the topic of “ECG Interpretation and Nursing Care”. Attendants are RNs from all over the country. The lecturers are Chatkanok Dumavibhat, M.D., Dr.Sarinrat Sriprasong, and Assistant Professor Thanistha Samai with other instructors from faculty members in Department of Medical Nursing and one RN expert from Siriraj hospital.
