Activities in 2016

Oregon Health and Science University (OHSU) Academic Visit

During January, 11th-14th, 2016, The Faculty of Nursing, Mahidol University had the honor of welcoming Dr. Mladenovic, Jeanette, Executive Vice President and Provost of Oregon Health and Science University (OHSU), United States of America , and Professor Dr. Bakewell-Sachs, Susan, Dean of School of Nursing, OHSU. The purpose of this visit is to discuss and finalize the plan for future academic collaboration between OHSU and the mentioned PhD program in Nursing.
