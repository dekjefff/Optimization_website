Teaching

The Department of Fundamental Nursing offers various programs including baccalaureate program, short course program of nursing specialty, and master’s degree program in gerontological nursing and nursing management.

The department also involves in teaching the other related courses for bachelor’s, master’s and doctoral programs. The teaching/learning activities include lecture, seminar, demonstration in nursing laboratory, and clinical practice in hospitals and communities.
