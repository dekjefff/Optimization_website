The Center of Excellence for Prevention and Caring for Persons with Depressive Symptoms was endorsed by the Department of Psychiatric and Mental Health Nursing in March 2008. The Center is dedicated to providing education, research, and nursing services to meet the challenges of prevention and caring for persons with depressive symptoms and their families.
