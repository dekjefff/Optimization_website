Doctor of Nursing Science Program (DNS)
Nurses are leading health professions. As an operative and researcher, they play an important role in the development of people's health. Finding problems and solutions to health problems of people in both at risk groups and groups with health problems in emergency and chronic crises; to synthesize knowledge and develop research leading to innovation in nursing and health service systems that respond to changing situations affecting health conditions both at the individual, family and social level according to moral, ethical and professional ethics perfectly and holistically.
