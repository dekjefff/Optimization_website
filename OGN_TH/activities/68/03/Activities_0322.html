Faculty of Nursing, Mahidol University Empowers Global Health Education with "IPE: Training for the Trainers" to Develop High-Quality Healthcare Professionals

22 มีนาคม 2568 ข่าวสารประชาสัมพันธ์

The Faculty of Nursing, Mahidol University, as a WHO Collaborating Center for Nursing and Midwifery Development, reaffirms its leadership in global nursing and health education by hosting the "Inter-Professional Education (IPE): Training for the Trainers" workshop from March 22 to 29, 2025. This initiative aims to enhance educators' ability to design and implement Interprofessional Education (IPE)—a key strategy for improving healthcare quality. Asst. Prof. Dr. Rudee Pungbangkadee and Asst. Prof. Dr. Puttiraporn Hungsawanus, faculty member of Obstetric and gynecological nursing, participated as the speakers.
